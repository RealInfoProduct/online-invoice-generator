EasyInvoice Help Page

Welcome to EasyInvoice, your one-stop solution for creating and managing invoices online. Our platform is designed to simplify the invoicing process, allowing you to focus on running your business.

Getting Started

To get started with EasyInvoice, simply sign up for a free account. Once you've confirmed your email and logged in, you'll be taken to the dashboard where you can create your first invoice.

Creating an Invoice

To create a new invoice, click on the "Create Invoice" button on the dashboard. You'll be prompted to enter the following details:

  • Client Information: The name and address of the client that you're invoicing.
  • Invoice Details: The date, invoice number, and due date of the invoice.
  • Item Details: A list of the items or services being invoiced, along with their quantities and prices.
  • Once you've entered all of the necessary details, click "Save" to generate the invoice. You can then download, view, print, or send the invoice directly from EasyInvoice.

Downloading an Invoice

To download an invoice, simply click on the invoice from the dashboard and then click the "Download" button. You can choose to download the invoice as a PDF or CSV file.

Viewing an Invoice

To view an invoice, click on the invoice from the dashboard. The invoice will be displayed in a read-only format, allowing you to review the details without making any changes.

Printing an Invoice

To print an invoice, click on the invoice from the dashboard and then click the "Print" button. The invoice will be formatted for printing, allowing you to easily create a physical copy.

Sending an Invoice via WhatsApp

To send an invoice via WhatsApp, click on the invoice from the dashboard and then click the "Send WhatsApp" button. You'll be prompted to enter the phone number of the recipient, and the invoice will be sent as a WhatsApp message.

Managing Invoices

From the EasyInvoice dashboard, you can view all of your invoices in one place. You can search for specific invoices, filter by date or client, and even mark invoices as paid.

Conclusion

EasyInvoice is designed to make invoicing simple and straightforward. With our easy-to-use platform, you can create, manage, and send invoices in just a few clicks. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.
